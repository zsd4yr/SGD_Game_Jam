You were hoping to hang out with your friends today, but it's raining! You'll have to stay indoors and keep yourself entertained.

GAME

Rained In is a fun-loving adventure game filled with object-collection, inventory management, and tons of minigames! Explore the house to collect items significant to Jimbo, his parents, and his big ol' pupper, Winston! Find the places where items belong to unlock entertaining minigames like Asteriods, Brick-Bracker, Duck Hunt, Hack n' Slash, The Ground Is Lava, Rubber Racer, Winston Goes Clubbing, Rained-In Tower Defence, Riddler, Tick-Tack-Toe, and the Amazing Maze!

This game was made in 48 hours as a part of the 1st Annual University of Virginia Game Jam, hosted by UVA's own Student Game Developers.

CONTROLS

E - select in overworld

WASD/arrow-keys - traversal in overworld

Minigames vary
